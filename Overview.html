Big City Recording is a full-service studio.  We offer the best in recording, mixing and mastering services.  Over our 20 year history we have recorded, mixed and/or mastered over 1,200 titles.


Our recording system is based around the Apogee Symphony system capable of recording 24 tracks simultaneously at sample rates up to 24 bit/192k.  The UV22 and SoftLimit functions onboard the converters ensure that the fine details of the recording are captured.  Coupled with the Apple MacPro Quad computer, this system is incredibly powerful.


We feature the Yamaha C7 Concert Grand Series piano.  This is a 7’6” length piano with a customized warm tone and very powerful low end.  We constantly keep the piano’s action maintained to factory specifications so we may provide you with the best possible performance.  Feel free to request a “test drive” and you’ll see what we mean!


We have a wide array of microphones including Neumann, AKG, Sennheiser, Shure, Sony, and Oktava among others.  Many of our mic’s have been customized by Jim Williams of Audio Upgrades.  We feature Universal Audio, Focusrite, Summit Audio, and Presonus analog processing.  Digital processing includes Altiverb, a comprehensive collection of UAD-1 plug-ins, Auto-Tune (graphical mode) and others to be used as necessary (we focus on mic placement and instrument fine-tuning first).


Our monitoring system includes Tannoy FSM (full scale monitors) speakers, Manley ML-10 mastering speakers, Yamaha NS-10’s, Klipsch home speakers, and a cheap boom box.  Monitoring is routed through a modified, passive Presonus Central Station, White speaker EQ, and Hafler amplifiers for true and accurate monitoring.
